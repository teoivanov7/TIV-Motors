AUDI RS7

The DAILY for YOU

❮ ❯

The King on the road

Expertly crafted by Audi's top engineers and designers, the stunning RS7 blends powerful performance with luxury, epitomizing cutting-edge automotive excellence.

The Audi RS7 boasts an impressive blend of high performance and advanced driver assistance systems. Its powerful 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine delivers 591 horsepower, enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds.
Alongside this performance, the RS7 is equipped with a suite of driver assistance features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a 360-degree camera system. These technologies enhance safety and convenience, ensuring a secure and enjoyable driving experience.
The Audi RS7 features a luxurious interior with premium materials like Valcona leather and carbon fiber accents, along with advanced technology such as the MMI touch response system and Bang & Olufsen 3D sound system.
Its exterior is marked by bold, aggressive styling, including a wide stance, signature Singleframe grille, and sleek LED headlights. The dynamic body lines and sporty rear diffuser give the RS7 a commanding presence on the road.
